N95 vs P100 for cleanup-related exposure concerns

People usually ask this when they want the shortest path to “what should I wear?” The real answer depends on the cleanup setting, time in the space, and whether dust is likely.

N95

Often the first reasonable choice for lighter cleanup scenarios where particle exposure is the main concern.

P100

Often chosen when someone wants a more robust respirator setup for heavier or longer cleanup work.

Missing piece

Either one works better when paired with ventilation, wet cleaning, gloves, and careful disposal instead of dry sweeping.
